about

I'm a program and product strategist with a scientist's curiosity and a builder's mindset. My background spans biomedical research, population health innovation, climate tech, and emerging technologies - but what drives me is making complex systems work better for people.

Currently exploring how AI can amplify human problem-solving, whether that's in precision medicine, climate resilience, or anywhere else innovation is needed. Learning to code and product design has opened up new ways to prototype solutions and bridge the gap between strategy and implementation.
